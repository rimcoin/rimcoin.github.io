SETUP

If you want a wallet, follow the instructions for your OS.

macOS

1. Click here, it will download a ZIP file.

2. Extract that.

3. There will be a lot of files. Push Command and Up Arrow.

4. Open Terminal.

5. Type the letters "cd" (w/o quotes).

6. Put a space, and drag in the extracted folder. And push return.

7. Type "cp wallet.py ~" (w/o quotes) and push return.

8. And now quit Terminal.

9. Re-open it and type "python wallet.py" (w/o quotes) and push return.

10. Type the letter n. And push return.

11. Type in your desired address. (where your money will be sent from and to) and push return.

You're done!

At any point, to access your wallet, do step 9, but type your address instead of n. And push return.

Windows

1. Click here, it will download a ZIP file.

2. Extract that.

3. Open that folder and run __RUNME.bat.

4. Type the letter n. Followed by enter.

5. Type in your desired address. (where your money will be sent from and to) and push enter.

You're done!

At any point, to access your wallet, do step 3, and type your address, and push enter.
